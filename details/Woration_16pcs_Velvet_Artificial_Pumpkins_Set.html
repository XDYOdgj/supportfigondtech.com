Woration 16pcs Velvet Artificial Pumpkins Set

Bring a touch of autumn elegance to your home with these beautifully crafted velvet artificial pumpkins. Designed for both indoor and outdoor use, they are perfect for decorating during Halloween, Thanksgiving, or any Harvest Festival. Each pumpkin in the set is made from high-quality velvet fabric wrapped around rigid foam, ensuring a realistic texture and lifelike appearance that enhances your seasonal decor with sophistication and warmth.
